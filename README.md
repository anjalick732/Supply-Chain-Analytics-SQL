# 📦 Supply Chain Analytics using SQL

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a supply chain dataset using SQL to identify business challenges related to inventory management, supplier performance, logistics costs, manufacturing quality, and revenue generation. The objective is to answer real business questions and provide data-driven recommendations that can help improve operational efficiency and profitability.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Supply chain managers often face challenges such as:

- Inventory shortages
- High logistics costs
- Supplier delays
- Product quality issues
- High manufacturing costs
- Inefficient inventory planning

This project uses SQL to analyze supply chain data and provide actionable business insights to support better decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze revenue generated by different product categories.
- Identify high-performing and underperforming products.
- Detect products requiring immediate restocking.
- Evaluate supplier performance using lead time and product availability.
- Analyze logistics costs across shipping carriers, transportation modes, and routes.
- Identify products with high manufacturing costs and defect rates.
- Generate business insights and recommendations using SQL.

## 📊 Key Insights

Some major findings from the analysis include:

- Skincare products generated the highest revenue and sales.
- Several products require immediate restocking due to low inventory.
- Supplier 1 had the shortest lead time, while Supplier 3 had the longest.
- Supplier 2 contributed the highest product availability.
- Carrier B, Air transportation, and Route B incurred the highest logistics costs.
- Multiple skincare products recorded high defect rates, indicating opportunities for quality improvement.

Detailed insights are available in **Insights.md**.
